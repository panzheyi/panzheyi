Short Bio

Dr. Zheyi Pan earned his Ph.D. degree at Apex Data & Knowledge Management Lab of Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2020, and got his B.Eng. degree from ACM Honored Class of Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2015, under the advisory of Prof. Yong Yu. His research interest lies in deep learning and data mining with a special focus on urban computing and spatio-temporal data. He has published several papers in top interational conferences and well-recognized journals, including KDD, CIKM, IEEE TKDE. He was an intern at Microsoft Research Asia and JD Intelligent Cities Research. He will join JD Doctor Management Trainee Program (DMT) in 2020.
